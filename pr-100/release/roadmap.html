Context#

The S-CORE v1.0 roadmap is structured around four release gates. Each gate has mandatory minimum deliverables; teams are free to advance ahead but the gate requirements are non-negotiable for the final release.

Hardening & Release

v0.8 establishes the baseline for the Time module. The v0.8 delivery comprises all work done to date — existing process artefacts in draft state, the existing CI setup, unit tests, and the reference_integration onboarding (#68 under PI #69) — plus any work completed before the gate closes on 13 Jul 2026.

This roadmap maps out a step-by-step plan to reach all v1.0 targets by December 2026, with v0.9 as the primary catch-up sprint for process artefacts.

Feature scope for v1.0 — features marked deferred have no valid_from ≤ v1.0 on their feat_req nodes and are automatically excluded from v1.0 coverage statistics. Their feat_req nodes and component artefacts (comp_req, comp_arc, comp_dd_sta) will progress through the full maturity cycle (L2+ → L4) in a subsequent PI.
